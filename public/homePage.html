Features

This app allows you to track your daily calorie intake, monitor your study time, and record all your login activities—including session length, activity type, dates, and personal notes. All your habits and routines are kept in one organized place, making it easier to understand your patterns and stay consistent with your goals.

Why use it?

A health tracker app is a simple but powerful way to stay organized and understand your daily habits. It helps you keep track of your calories, so you always know how much you are eating and can make better choices without guessing. It also lets you record study time and login activities—how long you spent, what you worked on, which days you logged in, and any extra notes. Having all this in one place makes it easier to see patterns, stay consistent, and improve both your health and productivity.
